Features:
Content ingestion
Toxicity detection
Auto moderation
Admin review
User warnings

Feature explanations (simple):
Content ingestion: The system collects incoming content (posts, comments, messages, images/text) from users so it can be checked.
Toxicity detection: AI analyzes that content and identifies harmful language/behavior (abuse, hate, threats, bullying, etc.).
Auto moderation: Based on toxicity score/rules, the system can automatically take action (hide, block, flag, or restrict content).
Admin review: Human moderators/admins see flagged content and make final decisions in unclear or serious cases.
User warnings: Users who post harmful content get notifications/warnings, and repeated violations can lead to stricter penalties.


Community platform: an online space where users share content, comment, react, and connect in groups.
UI style: feed-based layout (left menu, center content, right info panel), very similar to social apps.
User options: Home feed, Explore, Create post, Messages, Notifications, Profile, Saved, Report, Settings.
Which posts user can see: depends on visibility — Public, Followers only, or Group members only.
Posts vs Messages: post is broadcast to feed; message is private 1:1 or small group chat.
Like Facebook?: yes, concept is similar, but your platform adds AI toxicity detection and moderation.
